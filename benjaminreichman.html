Research

My research interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing and computer vision in multi-modal models. Specifically, I am interested in knowing how can we share knowledge between the different modalities to strengthen each of their performances. However, I am also interested in a variety of topics that are either exclusively in natural language processing (like summarization) or computer vision (like object detection methods).
